Only Ethical security researches can bring the bright light into your security.

Keeping your web applications secure is vitally important for business those days.

It's everlasting battle between criminals and security engineers.

No matter how much effort you put into system security, there can still be vulnerabilities present.

Responsible disclosure policy is a set of rules, which describes the ethical communication between business and security researcher, when vulnerability is found at your system or web application. It allows researcher to safely and privately report the issue to your team.

A responsible disclosure policy is the initial first step in helping protect your company from an attack or premature vulnerability release to the public.

Our mission is to protect you by sharing our research results and help you eliminate vulnerabilities
